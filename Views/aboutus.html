OUR STORY

In 2020 it started out as a fun project between five friends and would
later on in 2024 become a rising success. Second Chance to our
co-founders was a high school senior project and at that time they
couldn't have anticipated how important it would be to them.
After graduation they wanted to try to make the project idea real,
and since Egypt doesn't have thrifting stores like west it was easily
a unique idea that attracted many customers. A small business
started from there and the brand kept on growing attracting more people each day.
